# Disaster Management Portal
### Smart India Hackathon 2023

A web-based disaster awareness and preparedness platform that educates citizens about natural disasters, connects them with authorized response agencies, and facilitates community volunteering and donations.

---

## Features

- **Disaster Information Hub** вЂ” Detailed guides on Earthquakes, Floods, and Tsunamis with safety protocols
- **Interactive Quiz** вЂ” Test your disaster preparedness knowledge
- **Educational Videos** вЂ” Instructional content on emergency response
- **Community Forum** вЂ” Discussion space for disaster preparedness tips
- **Volunteer Registration** вЂ” Sign up to support disaster relief efforts
- **Donation Portal** вЂ” Contribute to relief funds
- **Disaster Statistics** вЂ” Data tables on historical disaster impact
- **Alert Popups** вЂ” Real-time emergency notification system
- **Authorized Partners** вЂ” Integrated with NDRF & SDRF logos and resources
